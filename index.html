Oyster and SeaStar nodes can be installed directly on strands, poles, cabinets, or exterior walls. These compact, low-power devices bring bandwidth to the building's edge without touching core pathways or structured wiring closets.

Key Benefits

  • Eliminates need for interior structured cabling rooms
  • Speeds activation timelines
  • Reduces construction and permitting risks
